About

I'm a multi-lingual (English - Spanish) software engineer who's passionate and dedicated to working with amazing teams. I'm always looking for opportunities to help with projects. I employ current standards used to meet today’s demands and technology uses.

I’m a lifelong self-motivated learner with strong organization, time management & communication skills. I'm able to work independently & collaboratively in a team with a meticulous attention to detail.

I hold a Full Stack Developer certification from Nucamp Coding Bootcamp where I became proficient in Bootstrap 4, React, React Native, Express, Node JS, and Mongo DB.

I continuously keep myself busy learning though completing challenging projects on the bleeding-edge of technology. I stay active on online communities such as Scrimba and Chingu, improving my skills daily and helping others.
